From pumpkins to reindeer, the holidays turn your yard into an obstacle course. Here's how Wicked Good Lawn Care keeps Alachua lawns festive and healthy.

If your yard looks like Santa's runway crashed into a pumpkin patch, you're not alone. Holiday inflatables, wire reindeer, plastic snowmen, and lighted gift boxes are fun, but they can turn your lawn into a full-blown obstacle course. And underneath all that holiday magic? You've still got living turf that needs to survive into spring. In Alachua and across North Florida, our grass doesn't completely "shut off" in winter. It slows down, but it's still alive and still affected by what's sitting on top of it.
