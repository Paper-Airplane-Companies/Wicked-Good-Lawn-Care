## Basic Recipes for Hydroponic Bounty

### Basil Pesto

**Ingredients:**
- Fresh basil leaves
- Pine nuts
- Garlic
- Parmesan cheese
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

**Instructions:**
1. In a food processor, combine basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese.
2. Pulse until finely chopped.
3. Gradually add olive oil until desired consistency is reached.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

### Sage-Infused Butter

**Ingredients:**
- Fresh sage leaves
- Unsalted butter

**Instructions:**
1. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat.
2. Add sage leaves and simmer gently for 5-10 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
4. Strain out sage leaves and transfer infused butter to a container.
5. Use as a spread for bread, topping for grilled meats, or seasoning for roasted vegetables.
